02. Roadmap

The Path

Phase 1: High School / Early College

Focus: GPA Foundation & Mechanical Identity.

  • Build manual hobbies (woodworking/mechanics)
  • Establish high GPA baseline (Biology/Physics focus)
  • Avoid burnout: Establish sleep/lift routine

Phase 2: The MCAT & Research

Focus: Academic Peak & Ortho Exposure.

  • MCAT Score Target: 515+
  • Email 3 surgeons for shadowing
  • Start one long-term research project (not just a poster)

Phase 3: Med School (M1-M3)

Focus: Anatomy Mastery & Networking.

  • Ace Anatomy & Biomechanics
  • Connect with PDs (Program Directors)
  • Honors in Surgery Rotation

Phase 4: Residency Match

Focus: Audition Rotations & Interview Skills.

  • The "Airport Test" (Be someone they want to work with)
  • Showcase durability and work ethic
